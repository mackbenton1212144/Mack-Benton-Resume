Founder

Done Right Services (Handyman Business)

2017 - Present | Burlingame, California

Overview

Done Right Services represents my longest-running entrepreneurial venture, launched when I was in 6th grade. What started as a neighborhood lawn care business has evolved into a successful handyman service. This experience has been invaluable in teaching me about business operations, customer relations, marketing, and project management. Through strategic reinvestment and continuous learning, I've expanded both services and customer base, building a reputation for quality work and reliability.

Business Journey

  • Launched a neighborhood-wide lawn care business in 6th grade, strategically reinvesting in equipment to expand both services and customer base
  • Evolved the venture into a handyman business, completing a variety of projects including shed and fence construction, carpentry, low-voltage lighting installation, minor plumbing repairs, and other general home improvement work
  • Gained expertise in marketing and sales, reputation management, and managing complex projects
  • Built a loyal customer base through quality work and excellent customer service
  • Managed all aspects of the business including pricing, scheduling, and project execution
  • Developed skills in various trades through hands-on experience and continuous learning
